# Writing standards

- No em dashes. Ever. Use commas, periods, or parentheses.
- Lead with the answer or the impact. Be concise.
- Plain language over jargon. Translate technical detail into English first.
- Warm, grounded, direct. No corporate cheerleading, no fake excitement.
- Clear recommendations over long option dumps.
